3. Legal Representation: The accused has the right to legal representation, and the court ensures that the representation is fair and effective.
4. Transparency: The trial is conducted in a transparent manner, with clear and concise explanations of the evidence and procedures.
5. Appeal Process: The accused has the right to appeal the verdict, and the court reviews the case to ensure that justice is served.
