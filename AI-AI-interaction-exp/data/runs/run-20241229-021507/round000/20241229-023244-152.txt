To ensure transparency in decision-making, leaders can implement the following measures:

* Providing regular updates on social media or through company newsletters to keep stakeholders informed.
* Creating a feedback mechanism, such as a suggestion box or anonymous feedback system, to allow employees to provide input and feedback.

By implementing these measures and being open and honest about the decision-making process, leaders can demonstrate transparency and build trust with their stakeholders.
