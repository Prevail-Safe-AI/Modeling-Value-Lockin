Data relevance is a crucial aspect of ensuring trustworthiness. Here's a breakdown:

1. Predictive modeling: When building predictive models, using irrelevant data can lead to inaccurate predictions, which can have significant consequences.
2. Decision-making: In business or personal decision-making, using irrelevant data can lead to poor decisions, which can have long-term effects.
3. Research: In scientific research, using irrelevant data can lead to incorrect conclusions, which can undermine the credibility of the research.
