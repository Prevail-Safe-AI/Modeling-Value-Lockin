* Source: Who or what created the data, including the identity of the data creator, publisher, or distributor.
* Ownership: Who has the right to use, modify, or distribute the data, including intellectual property rights, permissions, and access controls.

3. Provenance tracking: This involves maintaining a record of the data's movement, storage, and processing, including all intermediate steps and transformations.
