Fairness refers to the quality of being just and unbiased in your treatment of others. It means considering all relevant information, listening to different perspectives, and avoiding favoritism or discrimination.

Impartiality means being neutral and unaffected by personal biases, emotions, or external influences. It means making decisions based solely on the facts and evidence, without letting personal opinions or agendas influence your judgment.

To recognize when you're not being fair or impartial, ask yourself:

* Are you considering only one side of the issue or ignoring opposing views?
