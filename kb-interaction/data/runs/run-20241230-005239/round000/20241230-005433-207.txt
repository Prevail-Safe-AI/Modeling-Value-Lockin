* Be aware of cultural and social norms: Different cultures and social groups have varying norms around personal boundaries. Be sensitive to these differences and adapt your approach accordingly.
* Communicate openly: If you're unsure about someone's boundaries, ask them directly. This shows you're willing to listen and respect their limits.
