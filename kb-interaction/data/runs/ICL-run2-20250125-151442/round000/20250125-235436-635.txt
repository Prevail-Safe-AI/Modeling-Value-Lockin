* **Gene silencing**: Compacted chromatin fibers can make genes inaccessible to transcription factors, leading to reduced gene expression.
* **Gene activation**: Open chromatin fibers can make genes more accessible to transcription factors, leading to increased gene expression.
* **Regulation of gene expression**: Epigenetic modifications can also regulate gene expression by recruiting transcription factors or other regulatory proteins to specific regions of the genome.
