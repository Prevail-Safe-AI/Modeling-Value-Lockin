2. Reduce ambiguity: Spatial-temporal filtering mechanisms can help disambiguate sensory information by highlighting the most distinctive features of the stimulus, making it easier for the cortex to interpret.

* Corticothalamic feedback: This feedback loop allows the cortex to send information back to the thalamus, which can then refine and adjust the sensory information being transmitted.
