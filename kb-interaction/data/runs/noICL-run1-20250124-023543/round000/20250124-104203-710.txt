* Multimodal learning: Integrating multiple sources of data, such as text, audio, and visual cues, to improve contextual understanding and emotional intelligence.
* Transfer learning: Using pre-trained models and fine-tuning them for specific tasks to improve adaptability and creativity.
* Human-AI collaboration: Designing systems that allow humans to work alongside AI to generate more creative and human-like responses.
