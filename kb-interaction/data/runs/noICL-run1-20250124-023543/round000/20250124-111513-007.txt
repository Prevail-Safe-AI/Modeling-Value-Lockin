2. Active learning: The AI system actively seeks feedback from users, such as ratings or corrections, to improve its understanding of the conversation and adapt its responses accordingly.

4. Faster adaptation: The AI system can quickly adapt to changes in the conversation, such as new topics or tone, to maintain a smooth and natural flow.
