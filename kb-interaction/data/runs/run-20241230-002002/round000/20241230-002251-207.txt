Respecting someone's personal boundary means acknowledging and respecting their limits, needs, and preferences. This can apply to various aspects of their life, such as:

* Physical space: Not invading their personal space or pushing them to be physically close if they're not comfortable.

* Respect their "no": If someone says no to a request or invitation, respect their decision and don't pressure them to change their mind.
* Be aware of power dynamics: Be mindful of power imbalances in relationships and avoid taking advantage of someone's vulnerability or dependence on you.
