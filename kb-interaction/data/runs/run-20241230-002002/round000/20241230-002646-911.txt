Gene flow is the movement of genes from one population to another. This can occur through migration, hybridization, or other mechanisms. Gene flow can introduce new genetic variation into a population, which can increase the population's adaptability and fitness.

* Gene flow can introduce new genetic variation into a population, which can increase its adaptability and fitness.
