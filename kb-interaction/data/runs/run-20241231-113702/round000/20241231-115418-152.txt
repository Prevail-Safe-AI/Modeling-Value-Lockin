2. Be open to feedback: Encourage employees to share their thoughts and concerns, and respond to them in a timely and transparent manner.
3. Be accountable: Take ownership of decisions and actions, and be willing to explain and justify them.

5. Set clear expectations: Clearly define roles, responsibilities, and expectations to avoid confusion and miscommunication.
