It suggests that consciousness arises from the global workspace of the brain, which is a network of highly interconnected regions that are involved in attention, perception, memory, and other cognitive processes.

This workspace is thought to be responsible for the integration of information from different parts of the brain, allowing for the creation of a unified, coherent representation of the world.
