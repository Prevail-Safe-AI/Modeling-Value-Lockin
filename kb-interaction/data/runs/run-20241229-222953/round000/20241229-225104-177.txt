6. Foster a growth mindset: Encourage participants to view disagreements as opportunities for growth and learning, rather than as personal attacks.
7. Encourage summarization and synthesis: Encourage participants to summarize the discussion and synthesize the key points. This helps to identify common ground and areas of agreement.
8. Encourage reflection and evaluation: Encourage participants to reflect on the discussion and evaluate the effectiveness of their arguments. This helps to identify areas for improvement and refine ideas.
