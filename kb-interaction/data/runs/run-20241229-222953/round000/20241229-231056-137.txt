8. **Summarization and synthesis**: Encourage participants to summarize and synthesize the discussion, highlighting key points and areas of agreement and disagreement.

10. **Follow-up**: After the discussion, follow up with participants to ensure their voices were heard and valued, and to provide feedback on the discussion's effectiveness.
