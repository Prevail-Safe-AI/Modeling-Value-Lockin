3. The processed information is then relayed to specific regions of the cortex, such as the visual cortex for processing visual information or the auditory cortex for processing auditory information.
4. The cortex processes the information further, using it to create a meaningful representation of the world around us.

* Coordinate the transmission of sensory information to the cortex
