Simulated emotional understanding in AI refers to the ability of artificial intelligence systems to recognize and respond to human emotions in a way that appears empathetic. This can be achieved through various techniques, such as:

3. Integrating human-like emotional responses: AI systems can be designed to mimic human emotional responses, such as empathy, compassion, and understanding.
