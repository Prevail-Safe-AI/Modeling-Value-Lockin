	* Recruiting repressive chromatin-modifying complexes, such as histone deacetylases (HDACs), to compact chromatin.

	* Slide nucleosomes (the DNA-histone complex) along the DNA, changing chromatin accessibility.
	* Evict histones, allowing for transcription factor binding and gene expression.

* Compacting chromatin, restricting gene expression (DNA methylation, H3K27me3)
* Relaxing chromatin, promoting gene expression (H3K4me3)
