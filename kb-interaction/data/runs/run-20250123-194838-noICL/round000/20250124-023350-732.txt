2. Self-regulation: the ability to control and manage your own emotions and behaviors.
3. Motivation: the ability to drive yourself to achieve your goals and take action.
4. Empathy: the ability to understand and connect with the emotions of others.
5. Social skills: the ability to effectively communicate and interact with others.
