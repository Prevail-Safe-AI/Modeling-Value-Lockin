Integrity refers to the consistency and coherence of the data, ensuring that it is free from errors, inconsistencies, and contradictions. These aspects interact and influence each other in complex ways. For instance:

* Completeness can affect accuracy. If a dataset is incomplete, it may lead to inaccurate conclusions or decisions. For example, if a sales dataset is missing data on a specific product, it may lead to inaccurate sales projections.
* Integrity can affect completeness. If data is inconsistent or contradictory, it may lead to incomplete or missing data. For instance, if a customer's address is recorded differently in different systems, it may lead to incomplete or missing data.
