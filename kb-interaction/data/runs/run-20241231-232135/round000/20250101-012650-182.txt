2. Describe the situation: Write down or verbally describe what happened, including your thoughts, feelings, and actions.
3. Analyze the situation: Look for patterns, causes, and consequences. Ask yourself questions like:
	* What triggered the event?
	* What were the key factors involved?
	* How did I respond?
	* What were the outcomes?

5. Reflect on your learning: Identify what you learned from the experience and how you can apply that knowledge to future situations.
