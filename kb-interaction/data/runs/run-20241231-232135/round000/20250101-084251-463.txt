Consistency, accuracy, and completeness are indeed interconnected aspects of data quality. Here's a breakdown:

* Accuracy refers to the correctness of the data. For instance, if a customer's address is incorrect, accuracy is compromised.
* Completeness refers to the presence of all necessary data. For example, if a customer's phone number is missing, completeness is compromised.
