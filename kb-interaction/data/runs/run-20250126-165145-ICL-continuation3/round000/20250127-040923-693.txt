LTP is a long-term strengthening of synaptic connections, which can occur when neurons are activated together frequently. This strengthening can lead to improved communication between neurons and enhanced learning and memory.

LTD, on the other hand, is a long-term weakening of synaptic connections, which can occur when neurons are not activated together frequently. This weakening can lead to reduced communication between neurons and impaired learning and memory.
