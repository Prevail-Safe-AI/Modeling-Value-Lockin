Here's an example of a closed system where energy is conserved over time: a sealed container filled with a gas. * A car engine converting chemical energy from gasoline into mechanical energy.
* A solar panel converting sunlight into electrical energy.
* A refrigerator converting electrical energy into thermal energy to cool the interior.
